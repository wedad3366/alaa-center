About us

Trusted Immigration Experts for All Nationals

Alaa Center LLC is a trusted immigration services company with over 15 years of experience helping individuals, families, and businesses navigate the U.S. immigration system. With offices in New York, Cairo, and Dubai, we have successfully served thousands of clients worldwide.
We specialize in all U.S. immigration and non-immigration visa services, including family-based petitions, student and visitor visas, USCIS applications such as citizenship, work permits, and green card. Additionally, we provide comprehensive consular support for U.S. embassy cases, guiding clients through procedures, interview preparation, and document processing.
At Alaa Center LLC, we are committed to delivering accurate, ethical, and efficient services tailored to each client’s unique needs. Our team works with professionalism, transparency, and care, always complying fully with U.S. immigration laws and policies.

our services

K-1 Fiancée & K-3 Spouse Visas

We help U.S. citizens bring their fiancée or spouse to the U.S. through the K-1 and K-3 visa process, from petition to embassy interview. Contact us for more information.

Family Reunification for U.S. Citizens

We help U.S. citizens file immigration petitions (Form I-130) to reunite with their immediate and extended family members, including spouses, children, parents, and siblings — leading to a green card and lawful permanent residence. Contact us today to begin your family reunification process.

Family Reunification for Green Card Holders

We assist U.S. lawful permanent residents (green card holders) in filing Form I-130 to bring their spouse and unmarried children to the United States. Contact us to start your family reunification process.

Concurrent Filing for Green Card

Concurrent filing lets eligible applicants in the U.S. submit their green card application (I-485) along with work permit (I-765), travel document (I-131), and support forms all at once. This speeds up the process and allows for work and travel authorization while waiting. Contact us for help with your concurrent filing.

National Interest Waiver (NIW) Visa

The National Interest Waiver (NIW) visa allows eligible professionals with advanced degrees or exceptional abilities to bypass the usual labor certification process if their work benefits the United States. This visa is ideal for individuals whose employment in their field serves the national interest and who seek a faster path to a green card. Contact us to find out how we can assist you with your NIW petition.

USCIS

We handle a wide range of USCIS applications, including N-400 (Naturalization), N-600 (Certificate of Citizenship), I-131 and I-131A (Travel Documents), I-765 (Work Permit), I-750 (Removing Conditions on Residence), I-730 (Refugee/Asylee Relative Petition), and more. Contact us for expert assistance with your USCIS applications.

B1/B2 Non-Immigrant Visa

The B1/B2 visa allows individuals to visit the United States temporarily for business (B1) or tourism and medical treatment (B2). Whether you’re attending meetings, conferences, or traveling for leisure, this visa facilitates short-term visits with ease. Contact us to get assistance with your B1/B2 visa application.

Student Visa Services

We assist international students in obtaining U.S. student visas (F-1, M-1) to pursue academic, vocational, or language training programs in the United States. From application preparation to interview guidance, we support you throughout the entire visa process. Contact us to start your journey toward studying in the U.S.

E-1 and E-2 Investor Visa Services

The E-1 and E-2 Investor Visas allow foreign nationals from treaty countries—including Morocco, Tunisia, Egypt, Jordan, Bahrain, and Oman—to invest in and manage a business in the United States. These visas provide opportunities to live and work legally in the U.S. while growing your investment or conducting substantial trade. There is no fixed minimum capital requirement to apply for these visas. Contact us to learn how we can assist you with your E-1 and E-2 visa applications.

Consular Report of Birth Abroad (CRBA)

We assist U.S. citizens in obtaining the Consular Report of Birth Abroad (CRBA), an official document that certifies a child born outside the United States is a U.S. citizen. Our services include application preparation, document review, and guidance through the embassy appointment process. Contact us to help secure your child’s U.S. citizenship documentation.

U.S. Passport Services

We assist U.S. citizens with the issuance of U.S. passports, including first-time applications, renewals, replacements for lost or damaged passports, and expired passport renewals. Our team guides you through the entire process to ensure timely and accurate submission. Contact us for expert help with your U.S. passport needs.

Free to Marry Document Preparation

We assist clients in preparing the Free to Marry affidavit and other required documents for notarization by U.S. embassy consular officers. Our service ensures your paperwork is complete, accurate, and ready for the consular notarization process. Contact us for expert help with your Free to Marry document preparation.

Expertise in Affidavit of Support Forms

With extensive experience handling Forms I-864, I-864A, I-864EZ, and I-134, we assist clients in accurately preparing and submitting these important Affidavit of Support documents. Our team ensures full compliance with USCIS requirements to support family-based immigration and humanitarian petitions efficiently. Contact us for expert guidance on your Affidavit of Support forms.

Consular Paperwork Review Services

DV Lottery Visa Application Assistance

Our team guides you through every step—from paperwork preparation to interview readiness—ensuring a smooth path toward your U.S. immigrant visa. NVC Document Preparation and Interview Post Change Services

With extensive experience working with the National Visa Center (NVC), we assist clients in accurately preparing and submitting all required documents for their visa cases.
